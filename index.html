What We Do

Social Events

Trying to make sure every guest at your event is having a good time can be a stressful job, and where's the fun in hosting a party you can't even enjoy yourself? Let us handle the difficult task of picking a table centrepiece that isn't ugly and choosing a theme that isn't "80's Dance Party". Whether it's a wedding reception or a weekend party with co-workers, you can trust us to plan an event you'll be sure to remember for years.

Corporate Events

Planning exciting and engaging events for our corporate clients is our specialty. We will take care of all the details and ensure that your corporate team enjoys every second of it. Let us handle your event, from casual retirement parties to formal dinner events, to fundraisers and more!

Conferences/ Team Building

We will create a customized event that reflects your corporate branding or image and engages your entire team. Too often managers create events with no thought and no real goal in mind, which only wastes time and loses the respect of the team. Not here, we'll target whatever challenge you have and ensure your team returns to the office with inspiration and synergy.

Our Process

  • Meet & Idea
  • Design & Create
  • Implement
  • Review
This includes our first encounter—it's laid back and casual. We get a chance to get to know each other and understand your wants and needs, your budget, your deadline, what kind of coffee you like to drink, your general idea for the event and any themes or goals that you would like to have for your event. You have a chance to share any concerns you have and any suggestions you have yourself, and then we can take that information and start creating a concept.
How do you throw a party in outer space? You planet! This is the fun part. We'll take everything you gave us into consideration, add some sugar and spice, and work our magic to conceptualize, plan and create the best event you've never dreamed of. We'll cover everything from the perfect venue to making sure Vanilla Ice doesn't end up on the playlist. Throughout the process we will check in with you to make sure everything is up to your standards and nothing is out of place.
With us by your side, your event will run smoothly. We'll have the even covered from start to finish and make sure every guest is as comfortable and engaged as they can be. We'll make sure to have plan B ready in case there's any unexpected mishap, and if anything goes astray we'll be there to correct it right away!
