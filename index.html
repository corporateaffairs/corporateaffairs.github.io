What We Do

Corporate Events

Planning, organizing and carrying out spectacular and professional corporate events is our specialty. We take care of all the details and ensure that your team is organized and enjoying the experience. Let us handle your event, from a casual retirment party, to a team building event, formal cocktail parties or fundraisers.

Special Events

Corporate Affairs will create a customized event that reflects your style creativity and budget. Our events are creative stylish and fun. When you use Corporate Affairs, you will have access to our top level venues, caterers, photographers, DJs, and graphic artists.

Weddings

Corporate Affairs will customize your dream wedding. We will impecibly style your day and take care of every last detail, so all you have to do is enjoy the day. We offer full service wedding planning or wedding design and styling services.

Our Process

  • Meet & Discuss
  • Design & Create
  • Implement
  • Review
Our first meeting will consist of discussions that will help us determine what your needs and budget are so that we can formulate a plan for you event.
Corporate Affairs will put a proposal and itinerary together, that will review the details and costing of your event.
Corporate Affirs will be with you every step of the way. We will attend your event to ensure that the day runs smoothly and no details are missed, so all you have to do is enjoy the day.
Corporate Affirs will set up a meeting with you, the client, the week after your event to discuss concerns and areas of success which will help us plan your future events.
